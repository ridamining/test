RIDA WHITEPAPER

1. Decentralized Identity

RIDA Network utilizes UID-based hashing to ensure that every miner is unique and verified on the ledger without compromising privacy.

2. Mining Algorithm

The RIDA Node protocol operates on a low-energy consensus mechanism. It validates the state of the network every 24 hours to secure the distributed cloud database.

3. Distribution

Tokens are minted at a fixed rate of 0.20 RIDA per cycle. A halving event occurs every 12 months to maintain scarcity and protect token value.

4. The Ecosystem

The network will support a RIDA DEX and an NFT marketplace for hardware miners in Phase 3 of our roadmap (2027).
