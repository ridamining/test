RIDA NETWORK

Technical White Paper v1.4.0

1. Abstract

RIDA Network introduces a decentralized cloud-mining protocol that leverages mobile processing power to secure cross-chain transactions. Unlike traditional PoW, Rida utilizes a hybrid 'Neural Proof of Stake' (NPoS) to ensure energy efficiency and global scalability.

2. Network Architecture

The network operates on three layers: The Core Validation Layer (CVL), the P2P Distribution Layer, and the User Application Layer. Nodes are activated via the Rida Mobile Hub, allowing 24-hour cycles of validation that mint new RIDA tokens into the ecosystem.

3. Tokenomics & Halving

Max Supply: 100,000,000 RIDA tokens. The emission rate starts at 0.20 RIDA per cycle and will undergo its first halving event once the network hits 500,000 active verified miners.

  • 40% Community Mining Rewards
  • 25% Ecosystem Liquidity
  • 15% Development & Innovation
  • 20% Strategic Partners & Team

4. Roadmap to Mainnet

Following the Beta phase, RIDA will integrate with the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) for initial liquidity provision, followed by a native mainnet migration in Q4 2026. This allows users to swap mined assets for real-world value with near-zero gas fees.
